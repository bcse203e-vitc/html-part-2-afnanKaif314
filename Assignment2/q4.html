Exploring Chennai

Chennai, the city of love, is a must-visit for any traveler. With its beautiful landmarks, warm ambiance, and rich culture and heritage, there's something for everyone in the Tamilnadu capital.

Travel Tips for Chennai

  • Learn a few basic Tamil phrases; the locals appreciate it!
  • Book tickets for major attractions in advance to skip the lines.
  • Visit Beach early in the morning to avoid crowds.
  • Take boat ride along the Seine for stunning views of the city.
