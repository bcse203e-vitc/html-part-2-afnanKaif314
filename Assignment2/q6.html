Hydrabadi Biryani

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 200g rice
  • 100g chopped onion
  • 2 Eggs and chicken
  • 50g ginger garlic paste
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. marinate the chicken
  2. Fry the onion in a pan until crispy.
  3. boil the eggs
  4. cook the rice and add it to the marinated chicken.
  5. Season with black pepper and serve.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 500 kcal per serving
  • Protein: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 20g
